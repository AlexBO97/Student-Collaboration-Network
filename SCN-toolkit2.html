Ideas and Intellectual Property

Your idea is what drives your startup. Failing to record it properly and protect the intellectual property could lead to it being stolen and commercialised by someone else.
We offer a secure vault to store your idea, recording it with a time/date stamp for prior art purposes. Click on this link to take you to IdeasVault.

Don't let your idea be stolen!

Understanding the intellectual property you own and treating it as an asset right from the beginning is fundamental to growing your business value. We offer you instant overview of the opportunities and risks your assets contain, with essential information on how to take things forward. Click on this link to take you to Appraise.

The Intellectual Property Office has developed a set of resources to help Student researchers understand their IP better. Click on this link to take you to Cracking Ideas.

Funding

Acquiring funding as a student can be one of the most daunting tasks in getting your project off the ground.
The SCN has setup a dedicated Patch Page where students can crowdfund early stage money for their idea. Remember, the key to crowdfunding is offering rewards. Click on this link to take you to CrowdPatch.
