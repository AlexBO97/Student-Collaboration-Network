Our Toolkit




Your business idea or invention contains the intellectual property that drives your business plan.






You will need networks of people to help you grow your ideas and business. SCN and NACUE are student networks we help you to tap into, as well as two innovative new networking tools to showcase your ideas and meet experienced entrepreneurs, investors and partners.






Most startups will need funding to test their idea, grow their prospect base and protect their IP. Rewards and Equity based crowdfunding are the two best routes open to startups without finance in place.
