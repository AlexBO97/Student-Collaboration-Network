Hello, we are the Student Collaborators Network.

We are building an international network of students, with the goal to collaborate and achieve extraordinary things.

Collaborating with NACUE on startup and IP tools.

A little more about us.

Our story started with a chance encounter between a student and an entrepreneur. Together, we asked: "How could we get students access to this incredible scene, and get them the advise of the elders?".

The idea evolved, we decided to join forces to build a collaborative network to equip students with the skills and tools to start a business and protect the intellectual property that drives the business.

You will have to wait and see how the next chapter unfolds.
